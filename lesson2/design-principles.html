Design Principles - Jose F Hernandez

Proximity

Discover Puerto Rico

discoverpuertorico.com

In the principle of proximity, we see how the proximity of the object being displayed is changes to allow a focused view of what is being projected. The Discover Puerto Rico website has done a great job in illustrating what a visit to the island might look like.

We see the designer chose a picture that is zoomed out enough to cover s crystal clear sandy beach, as well as some coral reefs, palm trees and lush green forests. The capture to Discover Puerto Rico is centered to allow the viewer to discover what it is on this picture they would like to do on their next visit to this Caribbean destination.

Contrast

Ford

Ford.com

Ford does a great job in showcasing their products. By using contrast, Ford here matches the description of the truck model on the website by matching the blue color of the truck in the picture.

Ford uses the power of contrast to make the truck stand out from the picture as they are not interested on selling towable RVs, predict stormy weather or showcase a tree line.

By adding a contrasting color Ford makes their truck stand out in this beautiful picture.

Alignment

Apple

Apple.com
